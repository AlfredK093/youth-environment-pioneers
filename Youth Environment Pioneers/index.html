About

The Name of the organization is Youth environment Pioneers and the Acronym is 'YEPs'.

  • Background

    YEPs is a non-profit  organization established in pursuant to Non-Government Organizations Act of 2000(cap 5:05) of Laws of Malawi, in 2021. YEPs advocate for the natural environment management and utilization that aims to meet the needs of the present and future interests of generations.

  • As piooneers of the Natural Environment it provides legal represantation to the citizens being negatively affected to their environment, it engages in agricultural conservative program, a player in climate action, it plays an integral role in biodiversity conservation & Advocates for modern ways of waste management.                                                                                                       

  • It also advocates  for the people of Malawi to fully realize their right to a clean and healthy                                                         environment.        Our work corners through the core principles of environmental law, in promoting; Sustainability and intergenerational equity, public Participation and implementation of Polluter's Pay Principle, etc. 

  • Our Goal &

    Our Mission

    Citizens to fully realize their right to a clean and healthy environment                                                 To conserve, Protect and Sustain the Environment, and also promote enjoyment of a clean and healthy environment by the citizens of Malawi.
